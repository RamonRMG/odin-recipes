Magic Lasagna!

lasagna recipe

Delicacy made in ...

5 delicious steps!

  • Step 1: Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  • Step 2: Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
  • Step 3: Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  • Step 4: Spread an even layer of meat sauce.
  • Step 5: Repeat those layers two times.
  • Step 6: Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
